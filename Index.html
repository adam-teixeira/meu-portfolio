About me

I have a special affection for everything that connects technology and people. I'm a Computer Engineering student, an illustrator in my free time, and passionate about creating solutions that touch lives — whether through an app interface, a watercolor stroke, or a handmade assistive prototype.

I've developed projects born from personal questions that turned into bridges between people and possibilities: a pair of electronic glasses for people with visual impairments, a robotic hand for ALS patients, automation tools that support NGOs in their routines, and a UX-focused app for Russian restaurants in Brazil (yes, I'm also learning Russian!).

I believe empathy is the engine of innovation. I love listening to meaningful stories, exploring new cultures (I’m addicted to learning languages), and turning all of that into something useful, beautiful, and accessible.

If my work inspires you, reaching out would make my day. Welcome to my world — a place where code, art, dreams, and a genuine desire to do things differently come together!
